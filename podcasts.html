What it's about

Tim Ferriss is known as being one of the best podcast interviewers with his ability to not only find people at the top of their industry (which ranges from successful startup founder, to professional "fixer," to astrophysicists) but dissect how they got to where they are and find out what really makes these people excel at what they do.
